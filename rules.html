How To Play Battleship

Rules

The objective of Battleship is to try and sink all of the opponent's ships before they sink all of yours. Players take turns firing shots to attempt to hit the enemy ships.

When a player hits a tile that holds a ship, they can replay until they miss a shot.

First player to sink all the opponent's ships wins the game!

Controls

Before starting, you need to place all your ships on the board. To do that, you can:

  • Drag and drop the ships on the board (single click to rotate them)
  • Press random board to place your ships randomly

Once that is done, press start and try to defeat the AI!
